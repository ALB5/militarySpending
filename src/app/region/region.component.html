Spendings by region

Americas' first place is mostly due to the United States which amounts for 89% of the spendings of the region.

In Asia, China has a significant weight as it accounts for 50%. Just 12% for India.

There is much less imbalance in Europe. Russia, France and the UK are the greatest spenders of the region.

Due to the lack of data for a set of countries, conclusions for the Middle-East region are difficult to draw.
