The top 5 countries with the most increased in 2016

Latvia ranks first. It increased its spendings as it considers itself under the threat of Russia. The same explanation applies for Lithunia.

For Botswana, it seems a bit surprising, as the country is in a zone where there is no current conflict or direct threat. Nevertheless, the government engaged in a modernization programme.

The rise of the military budget in the Philippines aims at boosting its fight against Islamist groups and reinforce maritime security in the disputed South China Sea.

As for Mali, it pursues its effort to fight the Islamist extremist threat.
